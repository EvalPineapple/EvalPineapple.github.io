Privacy Policy for Eval Pineapple Chrome Extension

Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how the Eval Pineapple Chrome Extension ("Extension," "we," "us," or "our") respects and protects your privacy while using the Extension. The Extension is designed to replace the evaluation bar on Lichess and Chess.com with images of Grandmaster Hikaru Nakamura's faces.

Data Collection

The Eval Pineapple Extension does not save, share, or collect any user data. It neither collects personal information nor tracks your online activities. The Extension does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

Site-Specific Usage

The Extension operates exclusively on the Lichess and Chess.com websites. It does not access or interact with user accounts on these platforms. The Extension's functionality is limited to replacing the evaluation bar with images of Hikaru Nakamura's faces, and it does not perform any actions beyond this scope.

User Anonymity

We prioritize your privacy and anonymity. The Extension does not identify users or collect any information that could potentially be used to identify users. Your interactions with the Extension remain entirely anonymous.

Your Consent

By utilizing the Eval Pineapple Extension, you acknowledge your understanding of this privacy policy. Your use signifies your agreement to the outlined data collection and usage practices.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates. Your continued use of the Extension after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of such changes.
